About IT

What is IT

The field of information technology (IT) is expanding quickly and includes software development, network administration, cybersecurity, database management, and more.

You will have the opportunity to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills, both of which are highly sought after in today's job market, as an IT student.


Tips of Being a IT Student

    1. Read industry blogs, go to conferences and events, and follow industry leaders on social media to stay up to date on the latest technology trends.


    2. Develop your ability to solve problems by creating your own projects and experimenting with various technologies.


    3. Join online communities and work with peers to share knowledge and learn from experts in the field.


    4. Continuously learn and adjust to new innovations as the field is continually developing.
